Our Mission

We're on a journey to make rail travel predictable, efficient, and stress-free for millions of Indians

Trackwise was founded in 2018 with a simple goal: to eliminate the uncertainty of train travel in India. After experiencing one too many unexpected delays and missed connections, our founders set out to create a solution that would empower travelers with accurate, real-time information.

Today, we combine cutting-edge AI with a vast network of data sources to provide the most accurate train tracking and delay prediction system in the country. Our technology analyzes historical patterns, real-time conditions, and hundreds of other variables to give you reliable forecasts.

We're proud to serve over 5 million travelers monthly and partner with Indian Railways to improve operational efficiency. But most importantly, we're proud to give passengers back one of their most precious resources: time.

Our Journey

Key milestones in Trackwise's growth story

June 2018

Company Founded

Trackwise incorporated with a small team of 5 people working out of a co-working space in Bangalore.

March 2019

First Prediction Algorithm

Launched our first delay prediction model for the Delhi-Mumbai route with 78% accuracy.

November 2019

Seed Funding

Raised $2M in seed funding to expand our data collection network and engineering team.

July 2020

Mobile App Launch

Released our iOS and Android apps, reaching 100,000 downloads in the first month.

January 2021

Railways Partnership

Formal partnership with Indian Railways to integrate our predictive systems into their operations.

Present Day

National Coverage

Now tracking 10,000+ trains daily with 92% prediction accuracy and 5 million active users.
